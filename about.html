Vivek Shrivastava

Lawyer, Delhi High Court.

Vivek has a diverse knowledge base and expertise in the areas of law and management. He has done his MBA, LL.B and M.A in English. Previously he has been into academics and also worked as a business manager in technology firms in India.

Currently he is practising in Delhi High Court and helping start-up firms with legal and compliance support.

He loves to work with entreprenures and encourages problem solving realted to business and legal affairs.

Vivek strongly believes in fair workplace environment and helps employees in matters against public policy or employment laws.

Some of the major employees matters against employment laws are- Discrimination, Whistleblower Protection, Wrongful Termination, Wages and Hours, Sexual Harassment, Pregnancy Discrimination, Fraudulent Inducement etc.

Vivek also like to write articles and literary items, some of them can be seen at his BLOG

He loves connecting with people and sharing thoughts, ideas, attending seminars, confrences and guest lectures.
